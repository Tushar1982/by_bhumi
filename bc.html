Mental Wellness Strategies

Simple practices you can incorporate into your daily routine to improve mental well-being.

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, pause for 4. Repeat 5 times to calm your nervous system.

Mindful Meditation

Start with just 5 minutes daily. Focus on your breath without judgment to reduce stress and anxiety.

Hydration Check

Dehydration affects mood and cognition. Drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day.

Morning Sunlight

Get 10-15 minutes of morning sunlight to regulate circadian rhythm and boost serotonin levels.

Emotional Journaling

Write freely about your thoughts and feelings for 10 minutes daily to process emotions.

Social Connection

Reach out to a trusted friend or family member regularly. Social support is crucial for mental health.
